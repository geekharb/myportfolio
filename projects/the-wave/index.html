Project Year: 2019

The Wave

Project Sypnosis

The project started with a reflection of what an architecture for for cycling should be: a continuous surface where cyclists could roam freely. As a result, the initial exploration phase consists of an examination of how a single surface could be manipulated. This ultimately influenced the final form of the architecture.

The architecture also attempts to prioritise cycling activities and make cycling convenient for the cyclists, hence bicycle parking and bicycle lanes connects directly to various cycling related spaces.Â 

Finally, located on the rail corridor, the architecture seeks to become an iconic node along the railway corridor, and provides for recreational uses through the undulating surface.
