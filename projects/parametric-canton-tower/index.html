PARAMETRIC CANTON TOWER

Project Sypnosis

The Canton Tower is a 604m observation tower in Guangzhou, China. During its design process, a computational thinking process was adopted. The result is a coherent, elegant and structurally sound design solution.

This project attempts to reverse-engineer the computational logic behind the tower through its recreation. Elements of the tower, from the structural facade, the inner core to the ringed tops were computationally modeled using the Mobius Modeler, a Chrome-based computational design platform developed by the Design Automation Lab at NUS. Various variations were then generated through varying the parameters.
