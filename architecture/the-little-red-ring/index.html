Wrinkles

Project Synopsis

This project was submitted for the “Skin Design Competition” by the SIA.

We were inspired to re-imagine a facade that incorporates spaces and activities – an inclusive design regardless of age demographics, activities, needs, a skin which connects different floors in a vertical “Kampung”.
