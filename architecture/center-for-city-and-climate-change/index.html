Center for Cities and Climate Change

Project Synopsis

The discourse in environmentalism and climate change has primarily been between that of growth and sustainability. In city terms, efficiency manifests in the vast road networks, connecting people and goods to places of interest, while providing the fuel for economic growth. By looking at the transport networks of a city, one could derive its priorities.

The project for Center for Cities and Climate Change (C4) began by a thinking of the way we build our cities and our relation to the urban environment. The C4 seeks to connect the park connector to the park, providing convenience while forming a conversation with the public life. It is a statement for the way we build our cities.
